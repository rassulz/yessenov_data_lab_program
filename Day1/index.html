How to play

Think clearly before you fall

  1. Read the math problem written on the cave wall.
  2. Look at the three wells and the answers above them.
  3. Choose the one correct answer to survive the descent.
  4. If you are right, you continue to the next level.
  5. If you are wrong, the hero crashes and the game starts again.

Levels 1 to 4 use addition. From Level 5, subtraction begins. Levels 9 and 10 introduce binary numbers.

The lava glows below. Level 1 is waiting for the first brave answer.
